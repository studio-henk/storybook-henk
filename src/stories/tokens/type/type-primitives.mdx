### Font-size

These are the CSS custom properties (variables) for font-size in px. They are then used in <code>em</code> sizes in the font-scale calculations.
Sizes were derived from a modular scale called Major Third and uses the 1.25 multiplier.
See [Modular Scale](https://www.modularscale.com/?16&px&1.25).
